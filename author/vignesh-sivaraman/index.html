Vignesh Sivaraman

Assistant Professor

International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad

I am an assistant professor associated with the Computer Systems Group at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. I completed my PhD. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore, where I was advised by Biplab Sikdar.

Broadly speaking, I am interested in the network reliability, performance and privacy in various domains like Software Defined Networks, Information Centric Networks, Edge computing, Internet of Things, and Cyber-Physical Systems.

Interests

  • Network Security and Privacy
  • Internet of Things
  • Network Performance
